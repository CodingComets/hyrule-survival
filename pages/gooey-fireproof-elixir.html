Gooey Fireproof Elixir

I hope the sound of spontaneous combustion scared you enough to enlighten yourself in the ways of the wilds.

Here's an old reliable drink common among the folk of the foothill stable in the vicinity of Death mountain. It involves a type of lizard endemic to the region and an assortment of monster parts.

Normally. the recipe calls for a single Fireproof lizard and any monster part but we can do far better.

Personally, I enjoy the taste of Red Chu jelly in tandem with a single lizalfos tails and two complete Fireproof lizards for enhanced potency. Naysayers will dissaprove and claim that only additional servings of lizards will increase the effects.

While they may be right, why not treat yourself with a flavorful concoction with carefully balanced flavors? You may live in the wild, but the wilds need not live in you!

Ingredients:

  • 2 servings of Fireproof lizards
  • 2 servings of Red chu jelly
  • A single lizalfos tail

Preparation:

Slow cook on a bowl under a bonfire. Make sure to add the lizards, the jelly and the tail (or let pickle in bottle for added flavor).

Once boiling, pour content into a bottle. Enjoy your new found protection against the heat!
