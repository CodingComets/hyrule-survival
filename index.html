Evil rules the Kingdom

...and other realities of living in Ganon's Hyrule

Open your eyes! I know exactly what you're thinking; I can see it in that worried look of yours: "In a world full of monsters and ancient death machines, do we really need a cooking guide?

The answer to your questions is a resounding YES! Why wouldn't you want a cooking guide to help you survive in this post apocalyptic wasteland we call home? I bet you walked all the way to death mountain all decked up in a sweater only to be completely set ablazed as soon as you set foot on its fiery hills, subsequently becoming the well-roasted meal of a hungry, hungry lizalfos.

If this was you, don't be ashamed, we've all been there. So strap that sheikah slate and get your parasail ready because it's dangerous to go alone, and you'll need this:

  • Gooey Fireproof Elixir
  • Spicy Meat Curry
  • Sneaky Simmered Fruit
